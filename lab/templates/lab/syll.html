Physics II Lab

Course Title:
PHYSICS Laboratory II (01006726/01006703)

Course Credit:
PHYSICS Lab course [1 Credit or 20% from main lecture course]

Prerequisite:
PHYSICS I

Level:
Undergraduate

Semester/Year:
Second/2022

Mr. Ashan Kudabalage (Email: ashan.ku@kmitl.ac.th)

Course Description
● To practice the appropriate usage of measuring devices and understand the experiments which are correspondence with the theory of physics II.

Course Outline:

9.1 Objective:

To provide the students the knowledge of the use of scientific and electrical instruments.
To improve the students’ understanding of the main idea of each experiment which is correspondence with the theory in Physics II subject.
To improve the analytical skills of the students in solving a problems or finding solution to practical issues (Determine the problem, Research, Assumption/Hypothesis, Analyze, Finalize the method and Summarize the result).

9.2 Tentative Lab schedule:

Topic
1. Introduction to Physic laboratory II
2. Introduction to basic electronic devices and components I
3. Introduction to basic electronic devices and components II
4. I-V characteristics & resistor applications
5. Capacitors and its applications
6. Kirchhoff’s Circuit Laws
7. Semiconductor devices
8. The War of Currents: Why Use Alternating Current (AC)
9. Capacitors & Inductors (RC & RL circuits)

10. Procedure of teaching
● Describe the theory and the detail of each experiment
● Demonstrate an example setup for student understanding
● Conduct experiments related to the theory
● Lab assignment explanation at the beginning of the lab class (related to the previous lab experiment)

11. Lab Score distribution (Tentative)
● Lab Assignments 40%
● Quiz 10%
● Midterm exam 20%
● Final exam/ Project 30%

12. Reference:
● Hugh D.Young and Roger A.Freedman, University Physics with modern physics, 14th Ed., PEARSON Education, USA, 2016.
General usage policies of the Electronics Lab (If applicable):
● Any kind of Food or Drinks are not allowed at any time in the lab premises.
● No Furniture or Teaching Equipment is to be removed from this lab room.
● All the Furniture and Equipment here are for teaching / learning use only.
● Return back the equipment or the furniture in the proper position after using it.
● Vandalism and the writing on the computer table is prohibited.
● Playing or dancing or any kind of disruptive behavior inside the lab is prohibited.
● Students are not allowed to use the lab room for the personnel use such as party or any kind of personnel gatherings. However, students are allowed to use the lab for the experiment after the class time with permission by the lab supervisor.
● Please always lock the door and turn off the lights and air-conditioning after the class.
● Please make sure to inform to the lab supervisor any kind of damage caused during the experiments.
● Keep clean the environment around the lab premises.
